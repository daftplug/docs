## Overview

Related Posts uses AI to recommend relevant content to readers, keeping them engaged longer and reducing bounce rates. Unlike traditional related posts plugins that use simple keyword matching, AI understands context and semantic relationships.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Content Analysis">
    AI analyzes current post content, title, and topics
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Semantic Matching">
    Compares against all published posts using AI understanding
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Relevance Scoring">
    Ranks posts by true topical relevance, not just keyword matches
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Smart Selection">
    Chooses 3-6 most relevant posts
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Display">
    Shows related posts at end of content or in sidebar
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
**AI Advantage:** Traditional plugins match tags/categories. AI understands actual content meaning and context.
</Info>

## Matching Algorithm

### How AI Determines Relevance

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Semantic Understanding" icon="brain">
    AI understands meaning, not just keywords.
    
    **Example:**
    Current post: "Best React Hooks for State Management"
    
    **Traditional Match (Keywords):**
    - "React Hooks Tutorial" âœ…
    - "React Hooks Examples" âœ…
    - "Understanding React Hooks" âœ…
    
    **AI Match (Semantic):**
    - "Redux vs Context API Comparison" âœ… (related concept)
    - "Modern React State Management" âœ… (same topic)
    - "useState and useReducer Guide" âœ… (specific hooks)
    
    **AI Understands:** These posts are relevant even without "hooks" in title
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Context Awareness" icon="magnifying-glass">
    Considers full post content, not just title.
    
    **Factors:**
    - Post body content (all paragraphs)
    - Title and subtitle
    - Category and tags
    - Writing style and tone
    - Target audience level (beginner/advanced)
    
    **Result:** Better matches based on actual content
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Recency Weighting" icon="clock">
    Balance relevance with freshness.
    
    **Configuration:**
    ```
    Settings â†’ Related Posts â†’ Matching
    
    Recency Weight: 20% (0-50%)
    ```
    
    **How It Works:**
    - 80% relevance score
    - 20% recency boost for newer posts
    
    **Result:** Mix of best matches + fresh content
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Diversity" icon="shuffle">
    Avoid showing similar posts.
    
    **Strategy:**
    - Don't show 3 posts all about same specific topic
    - Vary between beginner/intermediate/advanced
    - Mix tutorial/opinion/news post types
    
    **Example:**
    If recommending React content:
    - Post 1: React Hooks tutorial (specific)
    - Post 2: React performance optimization (related)
    - Post 3: Building a React app (broader)
    
    Not all hooks tutorials!
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Performance & Caching

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Smart Caching" icon="database">
    Results are cached to avoid repeated AI calls.
    
    **How It Works:**
    1. First visitor: AI generates related posts (2-3 seconds)
    2. Results cached for 24 hours (configurable)
    3. Next visitors: Instant display (no AI call)
    4. Cache expires after duration
    5. Next visitor triggers refresh
    
    **Cache Duration:**
    ```
    Settings â†’ Related Posts â†’ Performance â†’ Cache
    
    Duration: 24 hours (1-168 hours)
    ```
    
    **Recommendations:**
    - **New blog:** 1-7 days (content changing frequently)
    - **Established blog:** 24-48 hours (balanced)
    - **Archived content:** 168 hours (rarely changes)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Manual Cache Refresh" icon="rotate">
    Force regenerate related posts.
    
    **When to Use:**
    - After publishing many new posts
    - After updating post content significantly
    - After changing matching settings
    
    **How To:**
    ```
    Generatify â†’ Tools â†’ Clear Related Posts Cache
    
    Options:
    - Clear all cached results
    - Clear specific post's cache
    - Regenerate all immediately
    ```
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
