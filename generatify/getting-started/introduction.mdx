## How It Works

1. **Install & Activate** - Get started in seconds with the WordPress plugin
2. **Connect AI Provider** - Add your API key from OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google
3. **Enable Features** - Turn on the modules you want to use
4. **Customize Settings** - Configure each feature to match your needs
5. **Let AI Work** - Sit back and watch AI enhance your site automatically
