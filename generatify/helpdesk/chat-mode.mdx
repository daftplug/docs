## Overview

AI Chat Mode provides instant, intelligent responses to visitor questions using your site content, products, and custom knowledge base. It's like having a support agent available 24/7.

## How AI Chat Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visitor Asks Question">
    User types message: "What's your return policy?"
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Context Gathering">
    AI retrieves relevant information from:
    - Your website pages
    - Product descriptions
    - FAQ content
    - Custom instructions
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Answer Generation">
    AI formulates accurate, helpful response based on context
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Response Delivery">
    Answer appears in chat within 1-2 seconds
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Conversation Continuation">
    AI maintains context for follow-up questions
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
**Smart Context:** AI automatically references previous messages in the conversation, allowing natural back-and-forth dialogue.
</Info>

## Configuration

### Enable AI Chat

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Settings">
    Go to **Generatify ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Helpdesk**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enable AI Chat Mode">
    Toggle **AI Chat Mode** to ON
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select AI Provider">
    Choose OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select AI Model">
    Choose model (GPT-4o, Claude 3.5, Gemini 1.5, etc.)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Knowledge Sources">
    Enable what AI can reference (posts, products, etc.)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Settings">
    Click **Save Settings**
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Knowledge Sources

Configure what information AI can access:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="WordPress Content">
    ### Posts & Pages
    
    **What's Included:**
    - Published posts (titles, content, excerpts)
    - Published pages (About, FAQ, etc.)
    - Post categories and tags
    - Post meta descriptions
    
    **Configuration:**
    ```
    Settings ‚Üí Helpdesk ‚Üí Knowledge Sources ‚Üí WordPress Content
    
    ‚úÖ Include posts
    ‚úÖ Include pages
    ‚¨ú Include drafts
    
    Limit: Latest 100 posts (performance)
    Categories: All (or select specific)
    ```
    
    **Use Cases:**
    - Blog content reference
    - Company information (About page)
    - Service descriptions
    - Policy explanations
    
    **Example Conversation:**
    ```
    User: "Do you offer web design services?"
    AI: "Yes! Based on our Services page, we offer custom 
         web design including responsive design, UX optimization, 
         and WordPress development. Would you like to know more 
         about our packages?"
    ```
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="WooCommerce Products">
    ### Product Catalog
    
    **What's Included:**
    - Product titles and descriptions
    - Short descriptions
    - SKUs and categories
    - Pricing information
    - Stock status
    - Product variations (size, color, etc.)
    - Product attributes
    - Product tags
    
    **Configuration:**
    ```
    Settings ‚Üí Helpdesk ‚Üí Knowledge Sources ‚Üí WooCommerce
    
    ‚úÖ Product info
    ‚úÖ Stock status
    ‚úÖ Pricing
    ‚úÖ Variations
    ‚úÖ Shipping info
    ‚¨ú Reviews (can be noisy)
    ```
    
    **Use Cases:**
    - Product recommendations
    - Availability questions
    - Specification queries
    - Comparison questions
    
    **Example Conversation:**
    ```
    User: "Do you have the blue t-shirt in large?"
    AI: "Yes! The Classic Blue T-Shirt is available in Large. 
         It's currently in stock for $29.99. The product features 
         100% cotton, relaxed fit, and free shipping on orders over $50. 
         Would you like the link to purchase?"
    ```
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="FAQs & Docs">
    ### Knowledge Base
    
    **What's Included:**
    - FAQ pages/posts
    - Help documentation
    - Troubleshooting guides
    - How-to articles
    
    **Configuration:**

## Conversation Settings

### Tone & Personality

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Response Tone" icon="face-smile">
    Configure AI's communication style.
    
    **Options:**
    - **Professional:** Formal, business-appropriate
    - **Friendly:** Warm, approachable, casual
    - **Enthusiastic:** Energetic, excited, exclamation points
    - **Concise:** Brief, to-the-point
    - **Detailed:** Thorough explanations
    
    **Configuration:**
    ```
    Settings ‚Üí Helpdesk ‚Üí AI Chat ‚Üí Tone
    
    Select: Friendly (default)
    
    Custom Instructions:
    "Respond in a warm, friendly tone. Use casual language 
    but remain professional. Add personality with phrases like 
    'Great question!' or 'I'd be happy to help!'"
    ```
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Response Length" icon="align-left">
    Control answer verbosity.
    
    **Options:**
    - **Concise:** 1-2 sentences (mobile-friendly)
    - **Balanced:** 2-4 sentences (default)
    - **Detailed:** Comprehensive explanations
    
    **Recommendation:**
    ```
    Mobile users: Concise
    Desktop users: Balanced
    Complex products: Detailed
    ```
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Follow-Up Questions" icon="question">
    AI proactively asks clarifying questions.
    
    **Enable for:**
    - Ambiguous queries
    - Multiple product options
    - Complex scenarios
    
    **Example:**
    ```
    User: "Tell me about your t-shirts"
    AI: "We have several t-shirt options! Are you looking for:
         - Men's or Women's styles?
         - Casual or performance fabrics?
         - A specific color?
         This will help me recommend the perfect fit!"
    ```
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Escalation to Human

Allow AI to transfer to human support:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Automatic Escalation Triggers" icon="user-plus">
    AI automatically offers human support when:
    
    **Trigger Conditions:**
    - AI confidence < 70% (uncertain answer)
    - User expresses frustration
    - Complex order issues
    - Refund requests
    - Technical problems AI can't solve
    - User explicitly asks for human
    
    **Configuration:**
    ```
    Settings ‚Üí Helpdesk ‚Üí AI Chat ‚Üí Escalation
    
    ‚úÖ Auto-escalate on low confidence
    ‚úÖ Escalate on user frustration
    ‚úÖ Escalate on refund requests
    
    Confidence Threshold: 70%
    ```
    
    **Example:**
    ```
    User: "I need a refund for order #1234"
    AI: "I understand you need a refund. To process this quickly, 
         I'd like to connect you with our support team who can handle 
         refunds. Would you like to submit a contact form or would 
         you prefer to email support@yoursite.com directly?"
    ```
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Manual Escalation Button" icon="hand-pointer">
    Always available option for users.
    
    **Configuration:**
    ```
    Settings ‚Üí Helpdesk ‚Üí AI Chat ‚Üí Escalation Button
    
    ‚úÖ Show "Talk to Human" button in footer
    
    Button Text: "Speak to a person"
    Action: Open contact form / Email link / Phone number
    ```
    
    **Button Placement:**
    - Widget footer (always visible)
    - After AI response (contextual)
    - After X messages (e.g., 3 messages)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Business Hours Integration" icon="clock">
    Show availability status.
    
    **Configuration:**
    ```
    Settings ‚Üí Helpdesk ‚Üí AI Chat ‚Üí Business Hours
    
    Mon-Fri: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM EST
    Sat-Sun: Closed
    
    During hours: "Talk to human support (available now)"
    After hours: "Leave a message (we'll reply tomorrow)"
    ```
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Conversation Flow

### Multi-Turn Conversations

AI maintains context across messages:

**Example Flow:**
```
User: "Do you have running shoes?"
AI: "Yes! We have several running shoe options. Are you looking 
     for men's or women's?"

User: "Women's"
AI: "Great! We have 5 women's running shoe models ranging from $79 
     to $149. Are you looking for neutral cushioning or stability 
     support?"

User: "What's the difference?"
AI: "Neutral cushioning shoes are best for runners with normal gait, 
     while stability shoes provide extra support for overpronation. 
     If you're unsure, our neutral models like the CloudRunner are 
     popular choices. Would you like to see them?"

User: "Yes please"
AI: "Perfect! Here's the CloudRunner Women's: [product link]. 
     It features lightweight foam, breathable mesh, and is $89. 
     Available in 6 colors. Need help with sizing?"
```

**Context Retention:** AI remembers:
- Previous questions
- User preferences mentioned
- Products discussed
- Issues being troubleshot

## Response Quality

<AccordionGroup>

### Cost Reduction Strategies

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Use Cheaper Models" icon="dollar-sign">
    Switch to lower-cost options:
    - Claude 3.5 Haiku (cheapest, still good)
    - GPT-4o Mini (fast + cheap)
    - Gemini 1.5 Pro (budget-friendly)
    
    **Savings:** 80-90% vs. premium models
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Limit Conversation Length" icon="messages">
    Set maximum messages per conversation:
    
    **Configuration:**
    ```
    Settings ‚Üí Helpdesk ‚Üí AI Chat ‚Üí Limits
    
    Max messages per conversation: 10
    After limit: Offer contact form
    ```
    
    **Prevents:** Endless back-and-forth costing money
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Smart Knowledge Indexing" icon="database">
    Only index most relevant content:
    
    **Actions:**
    - Limit to 50 most recent posts (not all 1,000)
    - Only active products (not archived)
    - Key FAQ pages only
    
    **Result:** Faster responses, lower token usage
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
